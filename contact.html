Frequently Asked Questions

Our subscription delivers fresh, locally-sourced food boxes every two weeks. Choose your box type, set your preferences, and we'll handle the rest. You can skip, pause, or cancel anytime with no long-term commitment.

We currently serve Moncton, Fredericton, and Saint John in New Brunswick. We're planning to expand to Halifax and Charlottetown soon. Delivery is available within city limits and surrounding areas.

Yes! When you sign up, you'll set your dietary preferences, allergies, and foods you love or want to avoid. Our team uses this information to curate each box specifically for your family's needs.

All products are sourced from farms within 200km and delivered within the same week they're harvested. This ensures maximum freshness and nutritional value while supporting truly local agriculture.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Payment is processed automatically before each delivery, and you can update your payment method anytime in your account.

Absolutely! You can skip individual deliveries or pause your subscription for up to 8 weeks through your online account. Just make sure to make changes before the weekly cutoff (Tuesday at midnight).

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with any items in your box, contact us within 48 hours and we'll provide a credit or replacement. We also use your feedback to improve future selections.

Yes! Gift subscriptions are available for 1, 3, 6, or 12 months. The recipient can set their own preferences and manage the subscription after the gift period. Contact us to set up a gift subscription.
