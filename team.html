Why We Started The Farm Basket

As four individuals passionate about sustainable food systems, we saw a gap between what families wanted and what was easily accessible. People cared about supporting local farmers and eating fresh, seasonal produce, but busy lifestyles made it challenging to shop at farmers' markets regularly.

Meanwhile, talented local farmers had amazing products but struggled with direct-to-consumer sales and marketing. We realized technology could bridge this gap, creating a win-win situation for both sides.

Our combined expertise in operations, strategy, product development, and business development gives us the unique ability to solve this complex challenge while building something meaningful for our community.
