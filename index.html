Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Farm Basket service.

How does delivery work?

We deliver bi-weekly via DoorDash/SkipTheDishes. You'll receive tracking information and can schedule your preferred delivery window.

Delivery is available Monday through Friday between 8AM-8PM Atlantic Time. You'll get 1-2 hours advance notice.

Can I customize my box?

Absolutely! Tell us about dietary restrictions, allergies, and preferences. You can also skip, pause, or cancel anytime.

Update your preferences anytime through your customer portal. Changes apply to your next delivery.

Where do products come from?

All products are sourced within 200km of your city from local Atlantic Canada farmers. Each item comes with farm information.

Every delivery includes farm stories and sourcing details. Meet your local farmers!

What if I need to cancel?

No long-term commitments! You can pause, skip deliveries, or cancel your subscription anytime through your customer portal.

Cancellations must be made 48 hours before your next scheduled delivery.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery is included in your subscription price. No hidden fees or additional charges.

We partner with DoorDash and SkipTheDishes to keep delivery costs low.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We guarantee fresh, quality products. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. We'll make it right!

Still have questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.
