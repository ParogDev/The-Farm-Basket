Our Mission, Vision & Values

Guiding principles that drive everything we do at The Farm Basket

Our Mission

To connect Atlantic Canadians with the freshest local produce while reducing food waste and supporting small-scale farmers within our communities.

Our Vision

A thriving Atlantic Canada where local food systems flourish, families eat better, and farming communities prosper through direct relationships with conscious consumers.

Our Core Values

Local First

Everything sourced within 200km - supporting our regional economy and ensuring the freshest possible products for your family.

Sustainability

Reducing food waste and environmental impact through thoughtful sourcing, perfect portions, and seasonal selections.

Transparency

Know your farmer, know your food. Every item comes with its story - from seed to your table.

Community

Supporting local economies and relationships that strengthen both farming communities and urban families.

Quality

Farm-fresh products delivered at peak freshness, with recipes and preparation tips to help you make the most of every item.

Impact

We're not just delivering food; we're building stronger local food networks that benefit farmers, families, and our environment.

Join Our Mission

When you choose The Farm Basket, you're joining a movement that's strengthening local food systems and building a more sustainable future for Atlantic Canada.
